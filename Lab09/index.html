Biryani

Created by Sudip Regmi

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas

Instructions:

  1. In a large saucepan, heat a little oil over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the ginger and garlic and cook for another 2 minutes.
  3. Add the spices and cook for 1 minute, stirring constantly.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes, chicken broth, and chicken to the saucepan. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. Add the frozen peas and cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Serve the biryani over the cooked rice.

This dish is my favorite since it is delicious, soothing, and simple to prepare. I like how the herbs and spices bring out the flavor of the chicken and rice's original ingredients. It's ideal for a busy weeknight meal or a spectacular event.
