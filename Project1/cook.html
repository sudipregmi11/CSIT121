Barbeque Chicken!

My favorite dish to cook is chicken barbecue. There's something so satisfying about marinating the chicken in a delicious sauce, grilling it to perfection, and then savoring the juicy, smoky flavors. I love experimenting with different marinades, ranging from sweet and tangy to spicy and savory.
